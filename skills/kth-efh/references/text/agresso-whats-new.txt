Handling attachments/documents

Add a new document: Click       and then click                          (Add document) in the bottom left corner.
Put the document in the folder “Bilagor”.

View attached documents: click         Change view by switching between A,
B and C. In A (full view) you can view pdf-files directly in the browser.
To view doc/xls-files. Change view to B or C. Right click on line/file and
choose “Skriv ut” (Print). Depending on browser settings the document will
now be saved or opened directly in Word/Excel.
(D presents same options as right click menu for marked document.)
