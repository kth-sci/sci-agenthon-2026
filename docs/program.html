Program

Plenary opening, two coding sessions, cross-track update after lunch, end-of-day live demos.

Morning Plenary · Salongen, KTHB

08:30 – 09:15 Arrival in Salongen (card-holders from 08:30; KTHB opens at 09:00)
09:15 – 09:20 Welcome and house rules (Fredrik Lundell)
09:20 – 09:45 Agents. What are they? (Artem Kulachenko)
09:45 – 09:55 Live demo (Artem Kulachenko)
09:55 – 10:05 Three-minute track introductions (each track lead)
10:05 – 10:15 Sub-team formation

Coding Session 1 · Track Rooms

10:15 – 10:30 Hands-on starter in each track room (track lead runs it)
10:30 – 12:00 Coding session 1 — sub-teams build their first deliverable

Lunch + Cross-Track Update · Salongen, KTHB

12:00 – 13:00 Lunch (lunch boxes catered to Salongen at 12:00)
13:00 – 13:15 Cross-track update — 3-minute status per track

Coding Session 2 · Track Rooms

13:15 – 15:45 Coding session 2 — sub-teams converge on a demoable result
15:45 – 16:00 Move to Salongen and set up for demos

Demos + Wrap-Up · Salongen, KTHB

16:00 – 16:45 End-of-day live demos — one short demo per sub-team (~10 min each)
16:45 – 17:00 Wrap-up, lessons learnt, next steps
17:00 Close

Track Details

Track A
Teaching

Course syllabus to teaching assistant

Two or more sub-teams in parallel: grading and feedback agents, student-facing Q&A agents, etc.

Lead: Artem Kulachenko, Jonas Sellberg • Room: Salongen, KTH Library (KTHB)
Track B
HPC & Research

Research and HPC workflow agents

Sub-teams pick a direction: orchestration on the cluster, agentic debugging, or an interactive post-processing agent.

Lead: Marco Laudato • Room: Faxén, Fluid Mechanics lab, Teknikringen 8, floor 1
Track C
Free Exploration Wei's Track

Open Agent Exploration: KTH systems, knowledge work, lab automation

Open exploration, no fixed tutorial. Wei will show a starter set of agent tools, then participants pick what they want to build. Administrative workflows (eISP, scheduling, document review), retrieval-augmented knowledge agents, lab automation.

Lead: Wei Ouyang • Room: Hållfasthetslära seminarierum, Teknikringen 8D, floor 1
